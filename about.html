We

Usually people come to Pizza Special just to eat. Our promoters distribute leaflets about a slice of pizza for twenty rubles or something else profitable. We are doing this as the first step to get acquainted.

But for us, Pizza Special is not just pizza. This is pizza, too, but first of all it is a big deal that inspires us, makes us wake up every morning and continue working with interest.

What is our interest? Let's tell you now.

Perfect ingredients

Why do we want to meet so much? Because then the pizza does everything by itself. People see that it is delicious, and come back again. The main thing for us is to show it for the first time.

In general, pizza is a very simple thing, it is difficult to spoil it. There are enough good ingredients and the right dough. This is a constructor, if the details are of high quality, then the result will be in order. Here they are, our details:

Dough

The most subtle thing. The main thing is to make it "alive". This is a whole quest of grams, degrees, percentages and hours with minutes. The process is not easy, but we are doing it!

Mozzarella

Cheese in pizza is a key ingredient. We use real mozzarella from Russian suppliers. The cheese should stretch like in the picture.

Filling

There are two main rules of a delicious filling: do not skimp on the filling; fanatically observe the storage mode. That's the whole secret.

Tomato sauce

The main thing you need to know about a good tomato sauce: it should be made from tomatoes. It sounds logical, but think about it!



Equally delicious everywhere

Anyone can make a delicious pizza. And the chef of an Italian restaurant will make a masterpiece at all. He's doing great. But imagine that you need to make a delicious pizza in hundreds of pizzerias, hundreds of kilometers from each other.

Pizza should be delicious and the same everywhere. Pizzerias should be equally cozy, cashiers should always be friendly, and couriers should be efficient.

And this is just difficult. But we can!

Common standards

Digital technologies help us to notice in time if something goes wrong. But how to understand what is right and what is not? We have standards for this. Here, for example, about hand washing:


  • coming out of the kitchen — wash your hands!
  • did the cleaning — wash your hands!
  • fixed your hair — wash your hands!
  • touched your clothes — wash your hands!
  • scratched your nose — wash your hands!
  • counted the money — wash your hands!
  • threw out the garbage — wash your hands!
  • sent a text message — wash your hands!



Our standards are not some abstruse schemes and formulas, but super-clear rules. We have hundreds of them, literally about everything. This is how we manage to do everything well and grow quickly at the same time.
